## Description
Portility lets you easily move conversations between AI assistants — Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini — so you can get a second opinion without starting over.

Copy a full conversation to your clipboard, summarize it with AI, or save it as a .txt file. Then open it in another platform with the right context already built in.

Features:
- Copy Conversation — extract and copy the full conversation text
- Summarize — get an AI-generated summary, then open it in Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini
- Save as .txt — download the conversation to your device
- Built-in content safety screening
- No account required

Currently supports conversations on Claude (claude.ai). ChatGPT and Gemini source support coming soon.
